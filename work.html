Public Webinar - Payatu

GRC That Grows With You - A Maturity-Based Approach to ISO 27001

Presented by Sudarson Prabhu - Live audience - GRC and information security community

Many ISO 27001 implementations fail not because of technical gaps, but because the framework is applied at the wrong scale. This session introduced a maturity-based model: right-size the programme first, then evolve it as the organisation grows.

Watch on YouTube
Watch on YouTube Starts at 05:32
Stage 1

The Kid Org

10 to 100 people. Informal and reactive. Focus on awareness and basic asset ownership. Do not over-engineer.

Stage 2

The Adult Org

Growing complexity. Multiple departments. Focus on a structured ISMS scope and centralised risk assessment.

Stage 3

The Mature Org

Security culture is embedded. GRC shifts from enforcement to advisory. Metrics drive decision-making.

Reframed GRC as a way to minimise surprises and enable confident decisions, not just produce documentation.

Explained why over-complicating at Stage 1 creates resistance, and under-structuring at Stage 2 creates chaos.

Positioned internal audits and management reviews as improvement engines, not compliance theatre.

Live Q&A: scaling compliance across departments with different risk profiles, and handling multi-subsidiary programmes.
