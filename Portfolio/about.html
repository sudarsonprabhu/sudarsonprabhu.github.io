Professional Summary

How I Work

Risk-led, audit-aware, and business-facing

I start by understanding the regulatory context, scope, control environment, and business objectives. From there, I turn requirements into policies, control design, evidence expectations, dashboards, risk treatment plans, and remediation pathways that are clear to both leadership and working teams.

I value clarity, traceability, and measurable follow-through. Whether the engagement is technical or governance-heavy, I aim to leave the organization with stronger structure, cleaner reporting, and a more mature security posture.
