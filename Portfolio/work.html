Work

Detailed view of the work behind the summary.

My portfolio spans governance, audit readiness, executive advisory, cloud security, and technical assurance work. This page breaks those responsibilities into clearer areas.

01

Regulatory audits, governance reviews, and risk reporting

I have worked on RBI-aligned regulatory system audits and governance-oriented assessments for BFSI and other high-assurance environments. These engagements typically require balancing control review depth with actionable reporting for leadership and compliance stakeholders.

  • Reviewed IT governance structures, cybersecurity controls, risk processes, and compliance posture.
  • Prepared risk-rated observations and remediation-focused audit outputs.
  • Supported internal audits, control effectiveness reviews, and compliance monitoring.
  • Contributed to management review and board-level reporting with structured security metrics.
RBI BFSI Internal Audit Compliance Monitoring Board Reporting

02

ISMS, accreditation readiness, and assurance programs

A major part of my work has been building and refining structured security programs that can stand up to external review, certification, and assurance requirements.

  • Led end-to-end ISO/IEC 27001:2022 implementations from scoping to certification coordination.
  • Performed risk assessment and treatment planning aligned with ISO 27005 and ISO 31000.
  • Developed Statement of Applicability, policy structures, control mappings, and audit support artifacts.
  • Supported readiness for ISO 17021, ISO 17020, ISO 17025, and SOC 2 engagements.
ISO/IEC 27001:2022 ISO 17021 ISO 17020 ISO 17025 SOC 2

03

Virtual CISO advisory and framework implementation

I have supported leadership teams in a Virtual CISO capacity, especially where the need is not only technical guidance but governance structure, reporting discipline, and clear security direction.

  • Provided vCISO support for Middle East clients across governance strategy and compliance oversight.
  • Supported DFSA cybersecurity framework implementation through gap assessment and policy guidance.
  • Helped define monitoring mechanisms, control maturity direction, and reporting structure.
  • Built KPI and KRI-oriented views to make progress and residual risk more visible.
vCISO DFSA Governance Strategy KPIs and KRIs Executive Advisory

04

Cloud security, detection, vulnerability management, and VAPT

Alongside governance and audit work, I have also worked in hands-on security operations and technical assurance contexts that strengthened my control design and remediation perspective.

  • Designed AWS security architectures with IAM, encryption, centralized logging, and segmentation.
  • Deployed and administered Wazuh SIEM and XDR for monitoring and event correlation.
  • Managed vulnerability lifecycle activities from scanning and prioritization to remediation tracking.
  • Supported incident response, phishing simulations, awareness efforts, and VAPT activities.
AWS Wazuh Incident Response Vulnerability Management VAPT
